About Us

MR AFRICA TOURS & SAFARIS

Whether you are coming to Zanzibar Island for a week or few days vacation you should always choose the best Zanzibar day trips and experiences like stone town tour, prison island tour, safari blue trip, mnemba atoll snorkeling tour, spice plantation tour etc.

Our daily activities consist of half day and full day trips and can be organized for individual, family and small groups, and we also have special services for big or incentive groups.

UNIQUE EXPERIENCES

We want you to have a one-of-a-kind and engaging experience, so we can take you to undiscovered jewels and add those extra touches to make things feel even more special.

TRUSTED LOCAL EXPERTS

Our qualified specialist tour guides have great understanding of the island areas and will be there to share local insight to make your holiday unforgettable.

24/7 AVAILABILITY

You're in good hands. So, if something unexpected happens during your tour, don't worry. We will keep you safe and informed. With our 24-hour helpline, wee are merely a phone call away.
